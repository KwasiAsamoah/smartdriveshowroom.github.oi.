Toyota RAV4 Prime


(known as the RAV4 Plug-in Hybrid in some markets). This is the high-performance, plug-in hybrid version of Toyota's best-selling SUV.

from GH₵55,000.00

Key Highlights
The RAV4 Prime is widely considered one of the best "all-around" vehicles because it combines sports-car acceleration with extreme fuel efficiency.
● Performance:
It produces a massive 302 net system horsepower. This allows it to go from 0 to 60 mph in just 5.5 to 5.7 seconds, making it one of the quickest vehicles in Toyota's entire lineup (second only to the Supra).
● Electric Range:
It offers an EPA-estimated 42 miles (68 km) of pure electric driving. For most people, this means daily commutes can be done without using a single drop of gasoline.
● Hybrid Efficiency:
Once the battery is depleted, it operates as a regular hybrid, getting an impressive 38 MPG (approx. 6.2 L/100km) combined.
● Drivetrain:
It comes standard with Electronic On-Demand All-Wheel Drive (AWD), using a dedicated electric motor for the rear wheels to improve traction.

Key Highlights
The i10 is a "city car"—smaller than the Logan but more agile and often better equipped with interior tech.

Its tiny footprint and light steering make it incredibly easy to park and maneuver through tight traffic or crowded souks.
